The Mission

One Platform. Total Automation.

Bajra Bots is an AI-powered automation platform designed to help small and medium businesses in Nepal save time by automating repetitive tasks using intelligent AI agents.

Our initial product focuses on fully automated social media content generation and posting. This allows businesses to maintain an active online presence without needing social media expertise or manual effort.

In the future, Bajra Bots will expand into a complete automation partner — allowing businesses to automate customer support via AI chatbots, lead management, and custom business workflows.
